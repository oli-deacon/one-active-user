Landing Brief exists because a loose pile of good ideas is not the same thing as a usable framing document.

Its job is not to write perfect copy. Its job is to force the right decisions in the right order, so the person using it can move from fog to structure without disappearing into a blank page.

That makes it a good example of personal software. It is not a generic copywriting assistant. It is a tool shaped around a repeated moment in one person&apos;s workflow.
