Strava Insights is a hand-built personal training workspace rather than a general fitness product.

The shape of the app is unusually specific: a single-user Strava connection, server-side token handling, recent activity import into MongoDB, and a set of pages that treat training data like something to be read, compared, and annotated.

What makes it feel bespoke is the mix of features around that core. The dashboard combines weekly distance, elevation, event timing, and run-category texture; the query page answers direct questions from stored data; and the Ask AI flow saves structured insights, workout sessions, and short progressions instead of leaving everything as disposable chat.
